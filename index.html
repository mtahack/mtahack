FAQ

What are the rules?

You are required to be a college/university student with a valid student ID card. Also, you must not start coding before the hackathon starts.

What do I gain?

A lot of fun, meeting new people, a chance to be recruited to some of the best companies, experience making a project and collaborating, a free T-shirt, free food and a lot more.

How is the hackathon going to be held this year?

With the recent vaccinations rolling out, we are planning to hold a frontal hackathon this year! If there will be any changes, then you will be updated immediately.

What if I don't have the required skills?

No need to worry about that, there is no minimum skill needed to participate!

How much does it cost?

Registration is free of charge!

What about stickers, shirts and swag?

There will be lots of it.
